User Guide

Quick Start

  1. Create an Account: Sign up with your email or link your Google account.
  2. Import Your Collection: Upload a CSV, paste your list, or scan cards with your phone’s camera.
  3. Build Your Deck: Use the deck builder to search cards, view suggestions, and check color balance.
  4. Analyze: Log games to see your win rates, draw percentages, and meta matchups.

Navigation Overview

  • Home: See recent decks, new sets, and announcements.
  • Collection: Manage your cards, see what you’re missing, and find duplicates to trade.
  • Deckbuilder: Build decks, view stats, and export to popular platforms like Moxfield or Archidekt.
  • Analytics: View game history, matchup breakdowns, and card performance ratings.

Tips & Tricks

  • Use the Price Tracker to get alerts when cards you want drop in price.
  • Tag decks as Casual or Competitive for more accurate suggestions.
  • Filter your collection by set, color, rarity, or foil/non-foil to organize trades faster.
  • Export your data regularly for backup, especially before large collection updates.

Troubleshooting

If pricing data fails to load:

  1. Check your internet connection.
  2. Clear your browser cache and refresh.
  3. Manually override prices by editing card entries.
  4. If issues persist, contact support at support@manaforge.com.

Contact & Support

Need help? You can reach us anytime:

  • Email: support@manaforge.com
  • Discord: Join our community server for deck tech help and bug reports.
  • Updates: Check the What’s New page for patch notes and upcoming features.
