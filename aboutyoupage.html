About Me

Hi, I'm Derek Aliff, and Magic: The Gathering has been a huge part of my life for over 14 years. From casual kitchen-table games to building competitive Commander decks, I've explored nearly every format the game has to offer. My favorite decks include Valakut Ramp and Hearthhull Land-Sacrifice strategies — decks that rely on clever resource management, synergy, and explosive finishes.

After years of scribbling decklists on paper and using multiple online tools, I saw how overwhelming it can be for new players to build a deck they truly enjoy. That challenge inspired ManaForge — an app designed to make deckbuilding easier, more intuitive, and more fun for everyone, no matter their experience level.

What Drives This Project

  • Passion for Accessibility: Helping new players build decks without frustration.
  • Strategic Depth: Sharing my love for synergy-based gameplay and clever combos.
  • Player Experience: Making tools that save time and improve decision-making.
